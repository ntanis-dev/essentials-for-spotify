  1. Login to Spotify for Developers with your Spotify account.
  2. Go to Create app.
  3. Set http://127.0.0.1: as "Redirect URI".
  4. Check "Web API" in "Which API/SDKs are you planning to use?".
  5. Fill the rest of the form to your liking, "Website" can be left empty.
  6. Create the app and navigate to the app's dashboard.
  7. Click "Basic Information" in the app's dashboard.
  8. Copy the "Client ID" to the form below.
  9. Click "View client secret" and copy the "Client secret" to the form below.
  10. Submit the form below.
